🔧 Technical Details & Innovation

🔬 Fraunhofer Institute Partnership

The renowned Fraunhofer Institute is currently finalizing the internal components of our first pilot plant in Pentacomo, Cyprus. The outer frame is already in place and commissioning is planned for late October/early November 2025.

Core Components of DESERTGREENER Technology:

  • 90% Energy Recycling: Our system continuously recycles 90% of the energy used in a closed loop. Only 10% additional energy input is required.
  • Modular Building System: "Like Lego blocks" - proven industrial components in new, highly efficient combination and reinforcement.
  • Dual Storage Solutions: Both water storage and electrical energy storage (accumulators) enable 24/7 operation regardless of sunshine duration.
  • Heliotropic Mirror Systems: Innovative mirror systems follow the sun's path and maximize energy absorption for seawater evaporation.
  • Mobile Units: Smaller, fully mobile devices developed for military use and disaster relief.

⚡ Energy Efficiency in Detail

Direct solar radiation is concentrated through our patented mirror system and used to evaporate seawater. The innovative recondensation process recovers a large portion of the energy used, creating an exceptionally efficient cycle.

🔬 Fraunhofer Institute Partnership

Scientific Collaboration at the Highest Level

The renowned Fraunhofer Institute for Building Physics (IBP) supports the development of DESERTGREENER technology with its years of expertise in solar water desalination.

🎓 Fraunhofer IBP - Solar Water Desalination

The Fraunhofer Institute for Building Physics has been researching in the field of solar-powered water desalination for years and has provided valuable foundational work for DESERTGREENER technology.

Project Progress with Fraunhofer IBP:

Phase 1: Feasibility Study (2018-2020)

  • Investigation of economic and technical feasibility
  • Analysis of operating parameters such as distillate production and technical performance
  • Consideration of feasibility of ETFE membrane mirror system
  • Increasing energy efficiency and protecting evaporator unit

Phase 2: Development and Proof-of-Concept (2021-2022)

  • Efficiency improvement of the plant under real conditions
  • Consulting on improving solar energy absorption and storage
  • Measurement investigations on performance
  • Identification of weak points and optimization opportunities

Current Phase: Cyprus Pilot Plant (2025)

  • Finalization of internal components of the first pilot plant in Pentacomo
  • Scientific support of commissioning and performance measurement
  • Optimization for series production based on pilot data
  • Quality assurance of technical standards

🔬 Scientific Breakthrough

The collaboration with Fraunhofer IBP was decisive in developing the 90% energy recycling system. The scientific foundation makes DESERTGREENER the world's leading technology in solar seawater desalination.
