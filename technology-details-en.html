The Vision: Recreating Nature's Water Cycle

"The sea is inexhaustible, the inland areas are drying up – so we must build the bridge between them."
— Karl Albrecht Waldstein

Desert Greener is not a classic seawater desalination project. It is the consistent implementation of an ancient idea: recreating the natural cycle of sun, evaporation and rain – locally, controlled and sustainably.

On the coast of Spain, this idea became a movement: a group of enthusiasts, not a large industry, but people with the vision to technically understand and harness the processes of nature.
